NETLIFY FORMS SETUP (JAMERYKA)

This site includes two Netlify Forms:
1) contact  (page: /contact)
2) schedule-request (page: /schedule)

After deploying to Netlify:
1) Go to your Netlify site dashboard.
2) Click Forms.
3) Click each form (contact, schedule-request).
4) Settings → Notifications → Add notification → Email.
5) Send to: jcc@jameryka.com
