Resources & Education

Required Notice: Resources are provided for general educational purposes only and should not be considered plan-specific guidance.

JAMERYKA provides educational resources designed to help individuals and professionals better understand health insurance coverage, responsibilities, and common processes. Information is subject to change and should be verified with official plan documents.

Resource Categories

Insurance Basics

Premiums, deductibles, copays, coinsurance, networks, referrals, prior authorizations, and claims.

Medicare & Senior Education

General concepts about Parts A, B, C, and D, enrollment periods, and coordination with other coverage.

Using Your Coverage

Understanding EOBs, pharmacy benefits, claims, appeals, and finding in-network providers.
