Insurance Brokerage Services

Required Notice: We do not offer every plan available in your area. Any information we provide is limited to the plans we represent.

What Brokerage Support Means

As a licensed insurance broker, JAMERYKA assists eligible individuals in understanding and enrolling in insurance plans that we are authorized to represent. Our role is to explain coverage options, answer general questions, and support enrollment where appropriate.

Types of Coverage We Support

  • Medicare-related plans (where licensed)
  • Individual and family health plans
  • Employer-based and supplemental coverage options, as applicable

How the Process Works

  1. Educational discussion
  2. Review of available options
  3. Enrollment assistance when requested
